METHANE

	Castle Methania
	Flatulencia in Excelsis

	for Seymour


	This is my fourth Tremulous map. It was designed to be monumental
	and vertical. I hope you enjoy it.

	This first alpha released is intended to test what I have build so
	far. A lot of corridors are still missing and the alien starting location
	is just temporary.
